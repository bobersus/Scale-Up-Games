Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 9, 2026

Welcome to ScaleUp Games ("ScaleUp Games", "we", "our", or "us").

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when visiting our website or interacting with our services.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you interact with our website, we may collect the following information:

Information You Provide:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Company or studio name
  • Any information you choose to include in contact forms or emails

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Operating system
  • Device information
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Referring website
  • Website usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Communicate with game developers and business partners
  • Evaluate potential partnerships and acquisitions
  • Improve our website and services
  • Analyze website traffic and performance
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized activity
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve website functionality
  • Analyze visitor behavior
  • Measure website performance

You may disable cookies in your browser settings, although some features of the website may no longer function properly.

4. Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services, such as Google Analytics or similar tools, to better understand how visitors use our website.

These services may collect information such as:

  • Device type
  • Browser
  • IP address
  • Pages visited
  • Session duration

This information is aggregated and does not directly identify you.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with:

  • Trusted service providers who help operate our website
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Professional advisors
  • Potential buyers or investors if ScaleUp Games undergoes a merger, acquisition, or sale

6. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services

  • Meet legal obligations

  • Enforce our agreements

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our website.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, including Roblox, Discord, X (Twitter), YouTube, GitHub, or other external services.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

10. International Visitors

Because our website is accessible worldwide, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your own jurisdiction.

By using our website, you consent to these transfers where permitted by law.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Request a copy of your information

To exercise these rights, contact us using the email below.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy
